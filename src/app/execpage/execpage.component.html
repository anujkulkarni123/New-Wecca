What is WECCA?

The Western Engineering Concrete Canoe Association, also known as WECCA, is a team of talented and driven students who come together each year to design and build a concrete canoe. Members participate in concrete mix design, mold development, and canoe theme creation. All this culminates in the Canadian National Concrete Canoe Competition where canoes from across the country are showcased and raced.
