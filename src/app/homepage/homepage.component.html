What is WECCA?

The Western Engineering Concrete Canoe Association, also known as WECCA, is a team of talented and driven students who come together each year to design and build a concrete canoe. Members participate in concrete mix design, mold development, and canoe theme creation. All this culminates in the Canadian National Concrete Canoe Competition where canoes from across the country are showcased and raced. Founded in 2007, WECCA annually competes at the Canadian National Concrete Canoe Competition. A team of Western University students come together to design, test, and cast a concrete canoe to be raced at the end of the school year. WECCA is continuously working to improve canoe design and performance to achieve future success. Canoe floats our boats, what floats yours?
